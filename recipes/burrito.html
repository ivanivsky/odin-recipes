Burrito

Description

Delicious recipe for a breakfast burrito

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients you need

  • Tortillas
  • Eggs
  • Sausage
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Onions
  • Jalapenos

Steps

  1. Cook the sausage
  2. Then toss in the eggs the last couple of minutes
  3. Then mix in the cheese
  4. Add all of that to the tortilla
  5. Toss in the onions, Jalapenos, and salsa
  6. Wrap up the burrito and toss on the grill for one for minute
